SnapNote Privacy Policy

SnapNote is a lightweight, local-first notes app. By default, your note content and attachments stay on your device; you can use the core capture features without signing in to an account.

Local-first storage

  • SnapNote stores note data on your device by default so you can capture, organize, and read content while offline.
  • In the current version, core data includes note title, body, tags, star status, archive status, and related on-device settings.

Optional iCloud sync

  • iCloud sync is optional. SnapNote uses Apple’s iCloud only after you turn it on.
  • The current version mainly syncs text note content, such as title, body, tags, star status, and archive status.
  • If you do not enable iCloud, sync does not start and the app continues in local mode.

Attachments

  • Images, documents, and other attachments stay on device storage by default and are not automatically uploaded with iCloud.
  • This supports a local-first experience and keeps attachment size, upload behavior, and network use easier to control.

How we use permissions

  • Camera: take a photo and add it as a local attachment to the current note.
  • Photos: pick an image from your library and add it as a local attachment.
  • Files: import only the files you choose through the system file picker; SnapNote does not scan your storage in the background.
  • Notifications: reminders, review nudges, or related alerts; we do not ask for everything at once on first launch.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: enable app lock and protect access to your notes on device. Biometric data is handled by the system; SnapNote does not read or store your fingerprint or face data.

Permissions on demand

  • SnapNote does not request every permission the first time you open it.
  • We ask only when you use the related feature—such as taking a photo, importing a photo, turning on reminders, or enabling app lock.

Your control

  • You can turn off camera, photos, notifications, or biometrics anytime in system Settings.
  • You can also disable iCloud sync or app lock in the app, and delete notes and attachments as needed.

Sharing and sale of information

  • SnapNote does not sell your personal information.
  • The current version is built local-first and does not rely on ads or accounts to deliver core note features.

Contact us

  • If you have questions about this policy or how SnapNote handles data, contact us at support@geeker.net.cn.
